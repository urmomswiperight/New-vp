# Email Marketing & Outreach Expert

This skill provides a tactical framework for high-conversion B2B cold outreach, focusing on infrastructure, deliverability, and psychological frameworks.

## Core Copywriting Frameworks

When drafting email or LinkedIn copy, prioritize these three frameworks:

1.  **PAS (Problem-Agitation-Solution):**
    - **Problem:** Identify a specific pain point (e.g., "Manual outreach is slow and hard to scale").
    - **Agitation:** Highlight the cost of inaction (e.g., "Your competitors are using AI to book meetings while you sleep").
    - **Solution:** Present your offer as the fix (e.g., "I've built an AI Agent that handles 5,000 leads/mo").

2.  **AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action):**
    - **Attention:** Hook with a personalized observation (e.g., "I saw what you're doing at {{company}}").
    - **Interest:** Share a surprising fact or result.
    - **Desire:** Show the benefit (ROI, efficiency).
    - **Action:** Clear, low-friction CTA (e.g., "Open to a 5-min chat?").

3.  **BAB (Before-After-Bridge):**
    - **Before:** Current state (Manual, fragmented).
    - **After:** Desired state (Automated, high-volume).
    - **Bridge:** How your service gets them there.

## Outreach Sequence (The 3-7-14 Cadence)

Follow this multi-touch schedule for maximum conversion:
- **Day 1:** Initial Outreach (Email + LinkedIn Connection).
- **Day 2:** First follow-up ("The Nudge").
- **Day 4-5:** Second follow-up (Share a case study or specific ROI).
- **Day 7-10:** Third follow-up (Social proof/Testimonial).
- **Day 14:** Fourth follow-up (Soft pitch/Value-add).
- **Day 21:** "The Breakup" email (Final check).

## Deliverability & Spam Avoidance

- **Infrastructure:** Always use separate domains for cold outreach (e.g., `robel-outreach.com` instead of your main brand domain).
- **Spam Words to Avoid:** "Free," "Guaranteed," "Buy now," "Cash," "Urgent," "Winner," "100%," "Investment."
- **Technical Setup:** Ensure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC are correctly configured for all sender accounts.
- **Rotation:** Use at least 3-5 sender accounts and rotate them to keep daily volume per account under 50-70 emails.

## LinkedIn Specific Tips

- **Personalization:** Use profile data (Current project, Recent post, Company mission) for the first sentence.
- **Tone:** Keep it "Social" not "Salesy." Use "I saw your profile..." instead of "I want to sell you..."
- **Daily Limit:** Stay under 25-30 connection requests per day to avoid "Account Restricted" flags.

## Usage Guidelines

- When asked to "Update a campaign," first analyze the current copy against PAS or BAB frameworks.
- Suggest subject lines that are lowercase and informal (e.g., "question about {{company}}" or "{{firstName}}?").
- Always include a "Unsubscribe" mechanism or a soft "opt-out" line.
