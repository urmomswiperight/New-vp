# AI Marketing Agent (15-Day Challenge)

An AI-driven outreach and automation system designed to secure one $500/mo recurring client within 15 days of launch.

## Vision
To automate the heavy lifting of B2B outreach (cold email/LinkedIn) for consulting agencies, focusing on Ethiopia/Africa (60%) and International (40%) markets.

## Core Features
- **Dashboard:** Usage, leads, campaign status (Next.js 15, Tailwind, shadcn/ui).
- **Leads Management:** Import 5000 contacts, filter, and view.
- **Campaign Engine:** Start/stop n8n-powered outreach (cold email + LinkedIn).
- **Billing:** Stripe subscription management ($500/mo, 5 uses or 7-day trial).
- **AI Onboarding:** Post-call competitor research automation.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Next.js 15, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui.
- **Backend/DB:** Supabase (Postgres, Auth, Realtime).
- **Automation:** n8n (Custom nodes for outreach/logic).
- **Payments:** Stripe.
- **Hosting:** Vercel.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] System live and functional.
- [ ] 5000 leads imported.
- [ ] Campaigns running on email/LinkedIn.
- [ ] **Goal:** One $500/mo Stripe subscription within 15 days post-launch.

## Target Audience
- B2B Consulting Agencies (small teams, growing revenue).
- 50% Ethiopia, 10% Other Africa, 40% International.

## Out of Scope
- Legal contracts.
- Magic link auth (standard email/password + Google OAuth).
- Paid advertising.
- Advanced analytics beyond basic metrics.
