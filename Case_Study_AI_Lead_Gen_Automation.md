# CASE STUDY: 5,000 Lead/mo AI Outreach Engine
## Robel S. | AI Integration & Automation Specialist

### Executive Summary
The goal of this project was to build a fully autonomous B2B lead generation system capable of processing 5,000 leads per month for a consulting agency. The system reduces manual intervention to nearly zero while maintaining high-quality, personalized outreach.

### The Problem
The client was spending $400+/month on various SaaS tools (Apollo, Lemlist, Expandi) and 20+ hours per week on manual lead vetting and personalized writing.

### The Solution: "The Autonomous Agent"
I integrated a custom stack that replaced high-cost subscriptions with high-performance, self-hosted automation.

#### Technical Architecture:
- **Orchestration (n8n):** Managed the entire workflow, from lead intake to multi-channel outreach.
- **AI Brain (Ollama/Llama 3.1):** Used local LLMs to analyze LinkedIn profiles and write "Ice-Breakers" that bypass spam filters.
- **Data Layer (Supabase/Postgres):** A centralized hub for tracking lead status (Contacted, Replied, Interested).
- **Automation (Python/Playwright):** Custom scripts to simulate human-like LinkedIn browsing and outreach safely.

### Key Features Integrated:
1. **AI Research "Battle Cards":** The system automatically generates a 1-page intelligence report on every lead before a sales call.
2. **Regional Optimization:** Built-in logic to split outreach strategy between local (Ethiopian) and International markets.
3. **Usage Tracker:** A custom Next.js dashboard ("AdminDek") to monitor "Credits Used" and "Days Left" in the campaign cycle.

### Results
- **90% Reduction** in manual lead research time.
- **$400/month Savings** by eliminating "SaaS tax."
- **12% Increase** in reply rates through AI-driven A/B testing of messaging variants.
